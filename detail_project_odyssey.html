Service

Shopify store design & development | UI | UX | HTML | CSS | JavaScript | Shopify Liquid

Client

Galaxy Odyssey

Year

2021

Summary

This responsive Shopify ecommerce website was designed for a company offering a single product, a high quality LED projector which displays stars, planets, moons and nebulas right onto the walls and ceiling. The goal was to develop a clean website which educated as well as sold the Galaxy Odyssey.

Process

When I started designing this project I knew I wanted big clear colorful pictures to showcase the beauty that this projector was capable of. I also wanted to demonstrate the various user cases for the projector, not just for bed time. I decided a testimonial section and some positive social media vibes from Instagram followers would give the product more credibility and show even more user cases and customer satisfaction. Another interesting aspect of this ecommerce website is the use of recommending related products that are also affiliate products available on Amazon. A great way to add additional income.
